Nine Grain Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Wets
  • 2.5 cups (5 sticks) butter - melted
  • 2 cups (432g) brown sugar - packed
  • 1/2 cup (108g) white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Drys
  • 3 cups (360g) wheat flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
Grains
  • 2 cups (240g) multi-grain mix, rolled
  • 2 cups (200g) rolled oats
  • 1.5 cups (186g) Grape Nuts
  • 1/2 cup (96g) steel cut oats
  • 3.5 cups chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Melt butter and mix completely with sugar in a large bowl. Add in remaining wets
  2. Mix together drys, then add to bowl with wets and mix thoroughly
  3. Slowly add grains until completely combined
  4. Bake at 375¬∞F for 14 minutes

Makes about 48 cookies (#24 scoop, 56g)
