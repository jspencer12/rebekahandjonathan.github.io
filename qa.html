Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Formal dress is requested.

The wedding party will be in black. If you'd like to coordinate with wedding colors, you're welcome to wear something from our sunset color palette of rich maroon, red, orange, and yellow pictured, with black as a compliment. For gentlemen, a black or dark suit with a tie in that scheme would look great, or dark khaki with a black tie. We will be so happy to see you no matter how you're dressed!

Are children welcome?

Yes! For both the Utah Reception and California 5k & Open House we would love to see your kids if you decide to bring them along. The venue is, however, indoors and not pet friendly.
